About Me

I wrote my first program many, many years ago during my freshman year of highschool. At the time I enjoyed it somewhat but didn't really get too into it. However my junior year I took an offered computer science cass and suddenly discovered that I had become addicted to programming. That class was the most fun I ever had in highschool and showed me exactly what I wanted to do with my life. A year later I was admitted to Rensselaer Polytechnic institute as a Comp. Sci. major. After my first few semesters here, I have already taken and, currently are taking several fun and interesting classes such as Data Structures and Computer Organization. My interests cover a broad range of topics from machine learning and ai to operating systems and I'm always looking to learn more about all aspects of computer science.

On campus, I spend my time doing a number of fun activities: I fence foil with the RPI fencing team, I attend CS club meetings, and I do CS homework! Next semester, I plan on participating in RCOS, the Rensselaer Center for Open Source Software, and working on a bunch of projects there. When I'm not doing work or programming or hanging out with friends, I greatly enjoy skiing. I've been skiing for as long as I can remember, roughly since I was six years old, and it is one of my greatest passions. I'm a member of the RPI Ski and Snowboard club and I go on many ski trips with family and friends. One day I hope to ski on every continent, except maybe antarctica.
